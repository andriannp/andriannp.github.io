Rangkuman Eksekutif Executive Summary
Andrian Nur Prabawa memiliki pengalaman selama 6 tahun sebagai tenaga pemasar tersertifikasi. Memiliki latar belakang pendidikan magister di bidang manajemen, saat ini memiliki peran sebagai ahli muda pemasaran dan penjualan bidang pengembangan dan strategi pemasaran di PT Kertas Padalarang atau dikenal sebagai Peruri Security Paper Mill (Perum Percetakan Uang Republik Indonesia Grup). Selain memiliki peran sebagai tenaga pemasar, saya juga merupakan Auditor untuk sistem manajemen mutu ISO 9001 dan sistem manajemen rantai pasokan ISO 28000. Telah berkontribusi dalam penerbitan karya tulis ilmiah berupa 1 artikel ilmiah yang berjudul "Understanding Consumer Intention to Use Indonesian Electronic Stamp Duty through Technology Acceptance Model (TAM): Study on PT Peruri Digital Security’s Consumer" dan 2 buah buku akademik yang berjudul "Pengantar Sistem Informasi Manajemen" dan "Manajemen Pemasaran (Perspektif Internet of Things)". Mendapatkan penghargaan kategori emas untuk stream inovasi pada ajang PERURI Innovation Award and Kaizen Award 2024 (IAKA 2024). Saat ini aktif sebagai anggota Indonesia Marketing Association (IMA) Chapter 106 dan pengurus Ikatan Keluarga Alumni Fakultas Ekonomi dan Bisnis Universitas Jenderal Achmad Yani.

Andrian Nur Prabawa has 6 years of experience as a certified marketer. With a master’s degree in management, he currently serves as a junior marketing and sales expert specializing in marketing development and strategy at PT Kertas Padalarang, also known as Peruri Security Paper Mill (Perum Percetakan Uang Republik Indonesia Group). In addition to his role as a marketer, he is also an Auditor for ISO 9001 quality management systems and ISO 28000 supply chain management systems. He has contributed to the publication of scientific works, including one scientific article titled "Understanding Consumer Intention to Use Indonesian Electronic Stamp Duty through Technology Acceptance Model (TAM): Study on PT Peruri Digital Security’s Consumer" and two academic books titled "Introduction to Management Information Systems" and "Marketing Management (Internet of Things Perspective)". He has been awarded the Gold Medal in the Innovation Stream category at the PERURI Innovation Award and the Kaizen Award 2024 (IAKA 2024). Currently active as a member of the Indonesia Marketing Association (IMA) Chapter 106 and as a committee member of the Alumni Association of the Faculty of Economics and Business, General Achmad Yani University.
